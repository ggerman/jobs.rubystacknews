- Design scalable APIs and data models

- Write high-quality, well-tested code

### What we’re looking for
- 7+ years of backend development experience
- Strong Ruby and Rails background
- Experience with distributed systems and production environments
- Solid knowledge of SQL databases
- Ability to work across teams and lead initiatives

### Nice to have
- Experience with event-driven architectures
- Exposure to cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or similar)
- Prior work on high-traffic systems
